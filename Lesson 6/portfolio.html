About Me

I'm Maya, a wildlife photographer based in Bristol with a deep passion for African safaris. I travel across savannas, wetlands, and reserves to capture the raw beauty and untamed spirit of animals in their natural environment; from the power of roaming elephants to the quiet elegance of big cats at dusk.

My work focuses on storytelling through the lens, highlighting the fragile balance of these ecosystems and the importance of conservation. I’ve exhibited locally and collaborate on projects that celebrate Africa’s incredible wildlife and landscapes.
